# Post-COVID Mental Health Survey

This is a **Post-COVID Mental Health Survey** created for students of **LAD Lady Amritabai Daga College**.  

The survey is designed to assess the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on college students’ **mental health**, including stress, anxiety, depression, social isolation, and study habits.

---

## 📄 About the Project

- Built with **HTML** and **CSS**.  
- Hosted on **GitHub Pages**.  
- Form submissions are sent directly to email owner 
- Fully responsive and easy to use.  
- Includes **16 questions** about mental health and online learning.
---
## 📝 Survey Questions

1. Name  
2. Age  
3. Gender  
4. Did you experience COVID-19 infection?  
5. Did you experience increased stress after the pandemic?  
6. Do you feel anxious about your future?  
7. Have you experienced sadness or depression?  
8. Do you have difficulty concentrating on studies?  
9. Has your sleep pattern changed?  
10. Do you feel socially isolated?  
11. Did online learning affect your mental health?  
12. Did you feel more academic pressure?  
13. Did you experience lack of motivation?  
14. Do you talk to others when stressed?  
15. Are you aware of mental health services in your college?  
16. Should colleges provide more counseling programs?

---

## 🚀 How to Use
---
1. Open the website link (GitHub Pages) in your browser.  
2. Fill in the survey with your details.  
3. Click **Submit Survey**.  
4. Your response will be sent directly to the project owner’s email.
